# Nova's PET 9-Week Study Plan
**Goal: Grade B floor / Grade A target | Start date: 2026-04-02**

---

## Baseline & Targets

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| Study period | 9 weeks (April 2 – June 1, 2026) |
| Weekly time budget | Weekdays: 2–3 hrs/week · Weekends: 4–6 hrs · Total: ~7–8 hrs/week |
| Total hours | ~63–72 hours across 9 weeks |
| Starting scores | Reading 19/32 (60%) · Listening 15/25 (60%) |
| Floor target (Grade B) | Reading ≥25/32 · Listening ≥19/25 |
| Stretch target (Grade A) | Reading ≥27/32 · Listening ≥21/25 |

---

## 4-Phase Structure

| Phase | Weeks | Focus | Pass Threshold |
|---|---|---|---|
| Strategy Install | W1–2 | Listening Part 3 framework + Reading Part 4 logic | L-Part3 ≥3/6 · R-Part4 ≥2/5 |
| Full Depth | W3–5 | All parts + Mock Test 1 (W3) + Writing starts (W5) | Reading ≥22 · Listening ≥17 |
| Sprint | W6–7 | Weak-point targeting + Mock Test 2 (W6) | Reading ≥25 · Listening ≥19 |
| Lock & Seal | W8–9 | Mock Test 3 (W8) + final consolidation | Reading ≥27 · Listening ≥21 |

**Design principle:** Each phase has a binary pass/fail threshold. If the threshold is not met, the next phase adjusts to address the gap before introducing new material.

## Spelling Sprint (W1–5, 5 min mid-week dictation)

- W1: Days of the week (Monday–Sunday)
- W2: Months (January–December)
- W3: Numbers in English (one–twenty)
- W4: Numbers (twenty-one–thirty) + location words (library, museum, stadium, theatre, gallery)
- W5: PET Part 3 high-frequency fill words (website, telephone, address, centre, class…)

**Rationale:** PET Part 3 is a form-filling task. Spelling errors on predictable words are preventable point losses.

## Writing Track (W5 onward)

| Week | Task | Time |
|---|---|---|
| W5 | Part 1 email framework lesson + first practice | 45 min |
| W6 | Part 1 second practice (within Mock 2) | 40 min |
| W7 | Part 1 third practice + Part 2 first attempt (narrative) | 60 min |
| W8 | Part 1 fourth practice (within Mock 3) + Part 2 second | 75 min |
| W9 | Final timed practice under exam conditions | 40 min |

**Part 1 email structure (3-element framework):**
1. Opening: salutation + state purpose (`Dear..., I'm writing because...`)
2. Body: respond to all 3 required content points from the prompt
3. Close: sign-off (`I hope to hear from you soon. / Best wishes, Nova`)

## Week-by-Week Plan

### W1 — Strategy Install ①
**Weekday sessions (Tue + Thu, 30 min each)**
- Tue: Listening Part 3 framework — use Past Paper 1 Part 3 script (no audio). Annotate each blank by information type (date / place / time / price / website). Build the pre-read → track sequence.
- Thu: Reading Part 4 logic — 8 items, 5 gaps, 3 distractors, each word used once max. Pronoun-tracking method. Logical connectives.

**Weekend (~5 hours)**
- Sat AM (60 min): Listening Part 3 — timed audio run, pre-read table, fill once, check script, re-listen to missed items
- Sat PM (45 min): Listening Part 2 error review — use Past Paper 1 Q8/Q10/Q13 scripts; trace how distractors work
- Sun AM (75 min): Reading Part 4 slow precision run — Past Paper 1, untimed, verbalize reasoning for every item
- Sun PM (30 min): Vocabulary check + spelling consolidation

**W1 pass check:** Can Nova state the expected information type for each Part 3 blank? Can she complete 3/5 Part 4 items using pronoun-tracking?

---

### W2 — Strategy Install ②
**Weekday sessions**
- Tue: Trainer 2 Test 1 — Listening Part 3 timed, self-mark, log error types
- Thu: Trainer 2 Test 1 — Reading Part 4, 2.5 min per item cap

**Weekend (~5 hours)**
- Sat (60 min): Reading Part 2 verification habit — re-do Past Paper 1 Part 2, confirm all conditions before committing to each answer
- Sat (45 min): Reading Part 4 second attempt (Trainer 2 Test 1)
- Sun (60 min): Listening Part 3 + Part 2 combined (remaining Trainer 2 Test 1)
- Sun (45 min): Reading Part 3 think-aloud — verbalize reasoning on Past Paper 1 wrong items

**W2 pass check:** Listening Part 3 ≥3/6 · Reading Part 4 ≥2/5 · Part 2 verification habit showing

---

### W3 — Full Mock ① + Error Analysis
**Weekday sessions**
- Tue: Reading Part 3 inference practice (Trainer 2 Test 1, 1 passage deep-read)
- Thu: Listening Part 2 — Trainer 2 Test 1, full distractor post-mortem

**Weekend (~5.5 hours)**
- Sat AM (90 min): **Mock 1 — Trainer 2 Test 2** (Reading 65 min + Listening 35 min)
- Sat PM (60 min): Mock debrief — score by part, log all errors into error bank immediately
- Sun AM (90 min): Weak-point deep dive (priority: Part 4 + Part 3 wrong items)
- Sun PM (30 min): Vocabulary + spelling review

**W3 target:** Reading ≥22/32 · Listening ≥17/25

---

### W4 — Targeted Repair (based on Mock 1 results)
Material: Trainer 2 Test 3. Focus on whichever Part missed its W3 threshold.

- If Part 4 still ≤2/5 → two dedicated Part 4 sessions this weekend
- If Part 4 ≥3/5 → shift focus to Part 5/6 vocabulary precision (word form, collocation)

---

### W5 — Full-Part Consolidation + Writing Starts
**Weekday sessions**
- Tue: Trainer 2 Test 3 — Reading Part 4
- Wed (bonus 45 min): Writing Part 1 framework lesson + first email practice
- Thu: Listening Part 1 — picture elimination: scan 3-image differences first, track only those during audio

**Weekend**
- Sat (90 min): Reading Part 2/3/4 combined — Trainer 2 Test 3 full reading section
- Sun (90 min): Listening Part 2/3 combined + writing second email

**W5 pass check:** Part 1 email structure internalized · Reading Part 4 ≥3/5 · Listening Part 3 ≥4/6

---

### W6 — Full Mock ② + Writing Part 1 ③
Same structure as W3. Material: Trainer 2 Test 4.
Writing Part 1 third practice embedded within mock.

**W6 target:** Reading ≥25/32 · Listening ≥19/25 (Grade B threshold)

---

### W7 — Final Gap Targeting + Writing Part 2 Starts
Weekday focus determined by W6 mock results.
Fixed items:
- Weekdays: Reading Part 5/6 — collocation precision, word-form discrimination
- Weekend: Writing Part 2 first attempt (narrative, ~100 words)

---

### W8 — Full Mock ③ (Full-Paper Conditions)
Material: Official Past Paper 2 Test 1 — all sections including Writing Part 1 + Part 2.

**W8 target:** Reading ≥27/32 · Listening ≥21/25 (Grade A threshold)

---

### W9 — Lock & Seal
**Principle: no new material.**
- Weekdays: Error bank review — focus on flagged uncertain items
- Weekend: Full vocabulary dictation test · Spelling final check · One Part 4 item to maintain form · Final timed writing practice under exam conditions
